Shengsen Wu

In 2022, I obtained M.S. degree at the National Engineering Research Center of Visual Technology (NELVT), Peking University, supervised by Prof. Ling-Yu Duan. In 2019, I obtained my B.E. degree in software engineering from Xiamen University.

My current research focuses on computer vision and autonomous driving, especially metric learning, object re-identification, and scene understanding.
